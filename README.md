<div align="center">

A lightweight Minecraft client mod that shows your **player rank, XP progress**, and adds a little flair to your HUD.  
Collect XP, level up, and see your rank grow from **None** to **Supreme**.  
</div>

**Features:**
- Animated HUD showing rank and XP bar  
- Rank icons above your head  
- Localized for multiple languages 
- Fully client-side, lightweight

**Dependences:**
- Fabric API
